About the Fest

The Qiskit Fall Fest is a global series of student-led quantum computing events, organized in collaboration with IBM Quantum and Qiskit. Every year, universities around the world host their own editions, creating a vibrant community where students explore, learn, and innovate together in the exciting field of quantum technologies.

At Shoolini University, our Fall Fest edition is designed to make quantum computing both accessible and practical. Through a mix of interactive workshops, hands-on labs, hackathons, and inspiring talks from mentors, participants gain a strong foundation in quantum concepts while applying them to real-world challenges.

From building quantum circuits and experimenting with Quantum Machine Learning to exploring error suppression techniques and advanced applications with Qiskit, the Fall Fest provides students with a unique opportunity to bridge theory and practice. It’s more than just a learning experience—it’s a chance to connect with a global network of quantum enthusiasts, develop new skills, and contribute to the growing quantum ecosystem.

This year marks a century of Quantum Mechanics—100 years since the revolutionary ideas that reshaped our understanding of the universe first emerged. From the pioneering work of Planck, Einstein, Bohr, and Heisenberg, quantum mechanics has evolved into the backbone of modern physics and the foundation for today’s cutting-edge technologies, including quantum computing.

At the Qiskit Fall Fest, we celebrate this milestone by exploring how a century of quantum discoveries has led to practical applications in Quantum Machine Learning, error suppression techniques, and real-world quantum computing with Qiskit. Participants will get a unique opportunity to connect the rich history of quantum theory with hands-on experiments and projects, understanding not just the “how,” but also the “why” behind quantum technologies.

By commemorating 100 years of Quantum Mechanics, the Fall Fest reminds us that every breakthrough starts with curiosity, creativity, and bold experimentation—qualities we aim to inspire in the next generation of quantum innovators.

FAQ

Is this beginner‑friendly?

Yes. Day 1 covers foundations, and mentors are available for help throughout the fest.

Do I need prior Qiskit knowledge?

No, we’ll provide starter notebooks and walkthroughs.

Will there be certificates?

Participation certificates will be provided to attendees who complete the workshops.

How do I form a team?

We’ll host a team‑forming mixer on Day 2 morning; solo participation is also welcome.

How do I report any issues or misconduct during the event?

If you witness or experience any misconduct or violations of the Code of Conduct, you can report it by sending an email to official_contact: quantumelitescholarssociety1@gmail.com or calling the emergency contact: +91-XXXXXXXXXX. All reports are handled confidentially.

Will there be networking opportunities with experts?

Absolutely. Qiskit Fall Fest offers several opportunities for participants to network with quantum computing experts, researchers, industry leaders, and fellow attendees.

Can I get help if I face technical difficulties during the event?

Yes. A dedicated support team and mentors will be available throughout the event to assist with technical issues or any questions related to Qiskit and quantum computing concepts.

Are there any participation fees?

No, the Qiskit Fall Fest 2025 participation is completely free of charge for registered attendees.

How will my personal data be handled?

All personal data provided during registration will be used solely for event management purposes in compliance with applicable data protection laws. Data will not be shared without your consent.

Can I participate remotely?

Yes. Qiskit Fall Fest supports remote participation. All key sessions, workshops, and challenges are accessible online via provided links.

Will I receive Qiskit Fall Fest Swags?

Yes! Registered participants who actively attend workshops and participate in challenges will receive exclusive Qiskit Fall Fest 2025 swags, such as event-branded umbrellas, water bottles, bags, stickers, and other goodies.

Do I get any financial support if I travel from outside to attend the event?

No. The Qiskit Fall Fest 2025 does not provide financial support for travel, accommodation, or any other personal expenses. Participants are expected to manage their own travel arrangements and costs. However, remote participation options are available for those who cannot travel.
